Design Process

1. Understand

Understanding the product, its users, and the problem like the back of my hand is pivotal to any project.

    Processes Include:
  • client meetings
  • problem identification
  • user research
  • industry research
  • comparative analysis
  • user interviews

2. Design

With the information gathered about the product and target users, sketch initial design solutions.

    Processes Include:
  • usability tests
  • affinity walls
  • personas
  • journey maps
  • wireframes & mockups

3. Iterate

Through the interviews and testings, gathere the users' pain-points and struggles of the designed interfaces. Edit, test, and repeat.

    Processes Include:
  • prototypes
  • client communication

4. Deliver

After the necessary iterations, deliver a high-fidelity prototype. Presentation of findings and processes if necessary. A follow-up with the client is important in ensuring service quality.
