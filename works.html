design consulting

This past academic year, I had the privilege of being a part of a software design consulting group called the UX Design Clinic.
My first contract-based app design consultation project! It was truly an amazing learning experience, and I've come to understand more of what happens behind the scenes of an app design process.

Client: Find Your Ditto

project completion: 04/07/2017

Parisa Soraya, the co-founder of Find Your Ditto came to us with a major design constraint in their existing mvp webapp. We began our journey with understanding the client and its product like the back of our hands.

Find Your Ditto

Understand.

Understanding the client's product and its users like the back of my hand was the biggest lesson I took away from this project. That's how we began our process.

  • user research
  • competitive analysis

Design.

With the information gathered about the product and target user base, we sketched our initial designs. Then the user testing and interviews began.

  • user interviews
  • affinity walls
  • low & med-fidelity prototyping

Iterate.

Through the interviews and testings, we gathered the users' pain-points and struggles of the designed interfaces. Edit, test, and repeat.


Deliver.

Since time was of the essence of the project's contract, we wrapped up the process by running a few iterative design rounds to come up with our high-fidelity prototype. Then came our final presentation in front of a crowd of clients, including our awesome client.

  • hi-fidelity prototyping
  • final presentation
