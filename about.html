About Me

I studied computer science in my earlier years in undergrad, and found that software development was fun and all, but there was only so much a developer can do to create awesome experiences for users. Then I found the School of Information offered a program to my new found passion, UX Design. Understanding the users is the best way to create better experiences for them. Now I'm aspiring to become a fullstack UX Designer that strives in highly functional and collaborative teams in the intersection of design and development.

I am passionate about designing interfaces that establish an emotional connection with the users. UI is to a person, as its UX is to his/her personality.
