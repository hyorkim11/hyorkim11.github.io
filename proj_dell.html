Laptop Comparison Tool

Client: Dell
Composition: Team(4) Design Project
Context: Senior Design(Capstone) Project
Goal: To enhance the usability of Dell's online laptop comparison tool

Skills Utilized

Software & Tools
  • InVision & Craft
  • Sketch3
Design Methods
  • Comparative Analysis
  • Heuristic Analysis
  • User Research & Interviews
  • Personas & Scenarios
  • Sketching & Wireframing
  • User Flows
  • Prototyping (Lo-Hi Fidelity)
  • User Surveys
  • Usability Tests

Description

The Order Experience team at Dell is currently exploring ways to make For-Home Laptops easier to buy. In line with this idea, they would like to test the user experience of the comparison feature, which allows users to compare two or more products, on Dell.com. For this project, the design team has been asked to study, analyze, and test the process of comparing laptops on Dell.com.

The overall objective of this project is to successfully deliver outstanding design solutions and recommendations to the client, in addition to addressing all the client-requested tasks in analyzing the existing solution. The team aims to deliver a high-fidelity prototype built on Invision to showcase the suggested design enhancements.

A successful project outcome would involve the following: Results and several recommendations based on usability tests and heuristic evaluation Working prototype of the comparison feature which takes into account the results and recommendations

Process

We began the project by conducting a comparative analysis of other laptop comparison tools existing on the web. This was done to understand what the consistent market standards are, in terms of designs and features they offer.
